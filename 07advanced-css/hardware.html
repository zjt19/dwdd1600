Hardware

Hardware is not necessary to use FL Studio by any means, but depending on personal preference it can help you interact with the program more easily. Here are some recommendations for keyboards or controllers you could use.

Keyboards

If you're used to playing the piano, it may be helpful to have that kind of interface to test instruments and play around different parts. But they're not just keyboards, they also have other controls that relate directly to FL Studio.

Controllers

This section focuses on controllers that are not designed to mimic instruments, but rather give physical inputs for FL Studio's many tools. These are good for anybody, regardless of experience with instruments.
