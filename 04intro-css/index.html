Home

Welcome to FL Music Help! This website is here to help people learn how FL Studio works and how you can create your own music with it. Not only that, but you can also discuss any tips you have to share or want to learn with others. Many DAWs exist, but I have the most experience with this one and I want to create a place for everyone to learn what I have.

There are multiple different pages on this website, so here's what you can expect from each of them:

Pages

Learn
This is where the bulk of the information will be. Within this page are multiple subsections that will help you understand the different aspects of FL Studio.
Discuss
This is where you can share your thoughts, comments, and tips as well as learn from others.
Links
This will simply have a list of links to Image-Line's website for your reference.
Hardware
Here you will find a variety of recommended controllers/keyboards that supplement the software. None of these are required to use FL Studio, but they can help.
About Us
Here, you can learn about the origins of this website and reach out to us via email.

Thank you for visiting our website! We hope that your time here is informative and enjoyable. If you'd like us to add anything, let us know!
